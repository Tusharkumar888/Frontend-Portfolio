Initiative - NextGEN CA

I am the founder of *NextGEN CA*, an initiative focused on the professional and personal development of Chartered Accountants. Through this platform, we aim to

  • Support underprivileged CA students to achieve their dreams of becoming Chartered Accountants.
  • Build a network of motivated CAs to bring about meaningful changes in the profession.
  • Empower members to adopt the *3P Vision: Prosperity, Productivity, Protection* to enhance their careers and lives.

Manifesto

Vision for ICAI Regional Elections 2024

As a candidate in the ICAI regional elections, my vision is to empower Chartered Accountants with skills that enable them to compete with top-tier MBAs from institutions like IIMs. My agenda focuses on

  • Enhancing *presentation skills* and helping members overcome stage fear.
  • Promoting *ethical advertising* for practitioners.
  • Tackling the *arrogance and corruption* encountered during departmental dealings.
  • Expanding professional avenues in response to the reduction in bank, tax, and CAG audits.
  • Advocating for *self-regulation* and resisting NFRA's encroachment on ICAI's disciplinary domain.
  • Reforming the *CABF*to ensure timely financial assistance for CAs in need.
  • Improving *study circle seminars*and introducing larger, impactful regional seminars.
  • Establishing grievance portals for *time-bound redressal*.
  • Creating sub-committees in taxation, audit, and accounting to boost member expertise.
  • Launching content writing and advocacy training wings to enhance representation at various levels.

Due Process in GST Cancellation Compliance

The issue in this case concerned the cancellation of Gayatri Steel Company’s GST registration without providing any reason and without giving the company an opportunity for a proper hearing.

Refund Claims Based on Transaction Substance

The petitioner, Cable and Wireless Global India Pvt. Ltd., filed for a refund of ₹47,33,053 related to unutilized Input Tax Credit (ITC) on the export of services.

GST Exclusion of Free Fuel Value

The issue in this case was whether the value of fuel supplied free of cost by the service recipient under an agreement could be added to the value of freight charges for calculating Goods and Services Tax (GST) under Section 15 of the CGST Act.

Refund Order with Interest and Penalty

The petitioner, Hallmark, filed for a refund of GST paid on goods returned during 2018. The initial refund application was within the limitation period, but the second application was filed after the department issued a deficiency memo, leading to a rejection due to a time-bar issue.

ITC Eligibility for Commercial Properties Under Section 17(5)(d)

The case challenges the constitutional validity of Section 17(5)(d) of the CGST Act, which restricts Input Tax Credit (ITC) for goods and services used in constructing immovable property intended for renting or leasing. Safari Retreats Pvt. Ltd. argued that denying ITC for such properties led to a break in the tax credit chain, creating an undue burden.

Rectification of Effective Date for Notification No. 54/2018

Cosmo Films Ltd. filed a petition seeking rectification of errors in a judgment regarding the applicability of Notification No. 54/2018-Central Tax. The main issue was the incorrect statement in the judgment that the notification took effect from October 23, 2017, instead of the correct date of October 9, 2018.
